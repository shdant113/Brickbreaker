Brick Breaker

Game adapted by Spencer Dant


Lives: 3 Level: 1

Game Over!

The object of this game is to brick all the bricks with the ball. You can try to steer the ball to break many bricks at a time or just go for each one. Either way, once you break them all, you win!

Use the left and right arrow keys to control the paddle, or use the a key to steer the paddle to the left and the d key to steer the paddle to the right.

To pause the game, press the pause button or press enter.

You Win!
